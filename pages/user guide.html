User Guides

Ensure the leaf or stem is clearly visible, well-lit, background contrast-free, and the entire affected area is captured. This will help the system analyze the image accurately and provide the best results.

Click the ‘Upload’ button, select your image, and wait for the processing. The system will analyze your photo and provide results shortly. Ensure the image is clear and of good quality to improve diagnosis accuracy.

Once your image is processed, you will receive the following information:

  • Disease Name: Identifies the plant disease detected.
  • Severity Level: Indicates if the disease is low, medium, or high in severity.
  • Recommended Actions: Includes suggestions like removing infected parts, treating the plant, or taking preventive measures.

Based on the results and recommendations, you may need to:

  • Adjust irrigation schedules
  • Apply fungicides or pesticides
  • Rotate crops or change farming practices
  • Consult an expert agronomist for further advice

Always follow up on the recommendations for optimal plant health and better crop yield.
